Disaster Safety Instructions

Flood Preparedness Actions

1. Move valuable items, livestock, and document packets to higher floors or platforms.

2. Stock up on water purification tablets, dry foods, and matches.

3. Never touch wet electrical sockets or walk through moving water.

Cyclone Preparedness Actions

1. Move to the nearest concrete Cyclone Shelter when signals reach levels 4 or higher.

2. Turn off the main electric switch and gas valves before leaving house.

3. Keep emergency radios and flashlights ready in dry plastic bags.

Earthquake Survival Actions

1. DROP, COVER, HOLD ON: Drop to hands and knees, get under sturdy furniture, and cover your head.

2. Stay clear of windows, tall wardrobes, and glass structures.

3. Do not run out of building doorways; falling brickwork/debris are high risks.
